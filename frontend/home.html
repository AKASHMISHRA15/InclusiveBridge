InclusiveBridge

InclusiveBridge is an intelligent monitoring system designed to assist patients and caregivers through real-time posture detection, facial expression analysis, and instant alert communication. It ensures safety, responsiveness, and seamless interaction between patients and distant family members or caregivers.
